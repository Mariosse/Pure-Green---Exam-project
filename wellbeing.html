WELLBEING
Here at Pickwick, we are dedicated to helping improve your wellbeing. With everyday life moving at such a fast pace, Pure Green is just what you need to help you through the day.

Read on to learn more about the health benefits of Pure Green.
ANTIOXIDANTS
It has been well established that tea, and in particular green tea, contains high levels of antioxidants. These can help prevent cell damage, which is especially important for maintaining a healthy and active lifestyle.
BRAIN FUNCTION
Sipping a cup of tea is great for relaxing but did you know that green tea can help with brain function? Studies have shown that green tea contains amino acid L-theanine, which works in combination with other properties in tea to improve your brain's capacity for focus and attention.
FRESH AND NATURAL
Pure Green ice tea is made with no added sugar or chemicals - just the highest quality all-natural green tea leaves and an invigorating burst of flavour. So whether you prefer a citrus twist or hint of floral, you can be sure you'll be left feeling refreshed and energised.
